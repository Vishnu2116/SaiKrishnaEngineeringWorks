Our Company

Established in 2000, Sai Krishna Engineering Works is an ISO 9001:2015 certified engineering company specializing in the design, development, and manufacturing of precision equipment for the explosives and detonator industry. With over 25 years of proven expertise, we deliver high-performance mechanical and electronic systems trusted by leading explosive manufacturers across India and abroad.

Our capabilities span the development of fuse combs, crimping units, dipping machines, drying tunnels, detonator testing equipment, VOD and timing instruments, safety-critical electronics, and custom-engineered components built to international quality and safety standards.

With strong in-house R&D capabilities, machining, electronics assembly, and precision fabrication facilities, we provide end-to-end solutions — from concept and prototyping to manufacturing and commissioning.
